Methodology

This indicator is a BondStats model based on the shape of the US Treasury yield curve. It uses the 10Y–3M and 10Y–2Y spreads as recession-related market signals.

The model is designed as an informational macro indicator and should not be interpreted as an official forecast or investment advice.
